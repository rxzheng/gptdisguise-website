# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 17/12/2025_

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our product or services.

## Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide access to paid features. This consists solely of an email address or login credentials used for account identification and payment verification.

No other personal information is collected. We do not collect usage data, analytics, device information, location data, or any content entered while using the product.

## How We Use Information

The email address or login details are used only for the following purposes:

- Verifying account ownership  
- Managing payments and subscriptions  
- Communicating essential service-related information  

This information is not used for marketing, tracking, profiling, or advertising.

## Data Storage and Security

Account and payment-related information is handled securely and accessed only as necessary to operate the service. When third-party payment processors are used, they are responsible for securely handling payment information under their own privacy policies.

We do not store full payment card details on our servers.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties, except where required to process payments or comply with legal obligations.

## Data Retention

Email or login information is retained only for as long as necessary to maintain the account or meet legal requirements. Users may request account deletion, after which associated information will be removed where legally permissible.

## User Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account information at any time by contacting us.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted here with an updated revision date.
